Welcome to OttawaJS.

OttawaJS is a group for Javascript and NodeJS enthusiasts in Ottawa. We meet to share information, demos, new technologies and promote client-side and server-side Javascript innovation in the local tech community.

Meetups

The OttawaJS group meets the 2nd Wednesday of each month at TheCodeFactory. We share information and ideas, show demos of our projects, ask questions and learn together. We try to have a couple of presentations each month that cover a variety of topics from beginner to advanced. Its a great place to meet people who are doing some very interesting projects in JavaScript.
